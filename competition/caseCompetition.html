Award-Winning Competition: 2020 Macy's Case Study

While at UW-Madison, my friends and I participated in a case competition hosted by Macy's. The focus
was on creating a brand strategy that focused on diversity and inclusion within one of their departments.


Our Idea

Our idea was to create an identity that spoke to the diverse consumer and appeal to this consumer through relatable anecdotes. By expanding personalization with diversity, this consumer can feel better represented within this segement and thus more willing to buy.
